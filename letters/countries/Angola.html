Here are some interesting facts about Angola

Did you know:

Angola, country located in southwestern Africa.
A large country, Angola takes in a broad variety of landscapes,
including the semidesert Atlantic littoral bordering Namibia’s
“Skeleton Coast,” the sparsely populated rainforest interior,
the rugged highlands of the south, the Cabinda exclave in the north,
and the densely settled towns and cities of the northern coast and north-central river valleys.
The capital and commercial centre is Luanda, a large port city on the
northern coast that blends Portuguese-style colonial landmarks with
traditional African housing styles and modern industrial complexes.

It has a population of 36,684,202
